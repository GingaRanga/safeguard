Paul Dixon
Owner/Operator

Paul Dixon spent 22 years in Retail Management in a variety of positions including Human Resources, Operations and General Management. In 2007 Paul and his Wife, Jody began to invest in rental properties, purchasing and managing 4 duplex. Having a keen interest in all areas of repair and maintenance Paul quickly took over all responsibilities of repairs and snow clearing while continuing a Management Career. In August of 2015 after many years of feeling the desire to be an entrepreneur, Paul finally took the leap towards starting his own business in Property Management and with his long-time friend and now Business partner, Mellisia Ansems-Ellis, Safeguard was born.

Mellisia Ansems-Ellis
Owner/Operator

Mellisia spent 12 years in Retail and service industry in a variety of positions including inventory control, logistics and Operations. In 2007 Mellisia began managing a property for a friend and by 2010 her and her husband Trevor purchased their first rental property. Also having a desire to be an entrepreneur, it was a natural fit to partner with Paul to start a Property Management company.
